Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods provide the option to add the cost or purchase price of simple product and variation product.

== Description ==

<p>Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods help you to find out your daily profit, weekly profit,monthly profit, yearly profit and total profit of your online web store/shop.</p>
<p>
<strong>Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods</strong> provide the cost of goods profit sales report to show the cost price or purchase price, sales price and product profit.
</p>
<strong>Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods</strong> provide report print and filter option to filter the order Today, Last 7 day, Last 30 day or this this year. 
<p>
<strong>Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods</strong> provide the dashboard sales analysis report like total sales, this year sales, this week sales and today sales.
</p>

1. Use the WordPress plugin installer or upload files to WordPress plugin directory.
1. Activate "Ni WooCommerce Cost Of Goods" plugin from admin panel.
1. Find Ni Cots Of Goods menu in admin.
